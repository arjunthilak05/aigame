📚Types of Machine Learning

Supervised Learning
The AI learns from labeled examples with correct answers. Like learning with a teacher! Examples: Predicting house prices, identifying spam emails.
Unsupervised Learning
The AI discovers patterns without labeled data. Like learning by exploration! Examples: Customer grouping, organizing news articles.
